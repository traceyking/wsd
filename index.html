What to Pack

You should always pack just incase an emergency should come up in your journey or if your vehicle breaks down.

  • Pack clothes according to weather conditions to your destination
  • Basic toiletries
  • Camping gear if you are planing on stoping at campgrounds. Such as sleeping bag tent small camp stove
  • Snacks and plenty of water
  • First Aid kit
  • Car emergency kit

Where to sleep along the way

  • Decide wether you want to stay in hotels the whole trip or if you want to find campgrounds along the way.
  • Make sure you research campgrounds and find out cost per night, amenities such as showers, fire wood, electric and local wildlife to watch out for.
  • If your staying in primarily hotels you should look to find the best deal and if you need a reservation prior.
